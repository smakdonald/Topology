Proposition 5.5.13.

Let n ≥ 1.
  1. Let Y := #n T2. (a-i) The space Y is homeomorphic to a quotient of a Euclidean 4n-gon with gluings labeled (counterclockwise) by a1b1a1-1b1-1 a2b2a2-1b2-1 ··· anbnan-1bn-1. (a-ii) π1(Y) ≅ \langle a1,b1, ... ,an, bn | [a1,b1] ··· [an,bn] = 1 \rangle. (a-ii) π1(Y)ab ≅ ℤ2n.
  2. Let Z := #n P2. (b-i) The space Z is homeomorphic to a quotient of a Euclidean 2n-gon with gluings labeled (counterclockwise) by a1a1 a2a2 ··· anan. (b-ii) π1(Z) ≅ \langle a1, ... ,an | a12 a22 ··· an2 = 1 \rangle. (b-ii) π1(Z)ab ≅ ℤn-1 × ℤ/2.
  3. T2 # P2 ≅ #3 P2
