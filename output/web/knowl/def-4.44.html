Definition 4.3.9.

Topological spaces X and Y are homotopy equivalent, written X \simeq Y, if there are continuous functions f:X \to Y and g:Y \to X such that f \circ g \simeq 1Y and g \circ f \simeq 1X. The functions f and g are called homotopy equivalences, and each function is a homotopy inverse of the other.
