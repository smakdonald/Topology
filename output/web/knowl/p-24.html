Discussion of standard mathematical conventions for notation Notation for indices: \(i,j,k,m,n\) for finite or countably many indices, \(α,β,...\) for arbitrarily many indices. Notation for sets, elements of sets, and sets of sets: Small letters \(a,b,c,p,q,x,y,z\) for elements of sets, capital letters \(A,B,C,X,Y,Z\) for sets, calligraphic capital letters \(ℬ,𝒞,𝒯,𝒰\) for collections of sets.
