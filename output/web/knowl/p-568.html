Let n \geq 1.
  1. Let Y := #n T^2. (a-i) The space Y is homeomorphic to a quotient of a Euclidean 4n-gon with gluings labeled (counterclockwise) by a1b1a1-1b1-1 a2b2a2-1b2-1 ··· anbnan-1bn-1. (a-ii) \pi_1(Y) \cong \langle a1,b1, \dots,an, bn | [a1,b1] ··· [an,bn] = 1 \rangle. (a-ii) \pi_1(Y)ab \cong \Z 2n.
  2. Let Z := #n P^2. (b-i) The space Z is homeomorphic to a quotient of a Euclidean 2n-gon with gluings labeled (counterclockwise) by a1a1 a2a2 ··· anan. (b-ii) \pi_1(Z) \cong \langle a1, \dots,an | a12 a22 ··· an2 = 1 \rangle. (b-ii) \pi_1(Z)ab \cong \Z n-1 \times \Z /2.
  3. T^2 # P^2 \cong #3 P^2
