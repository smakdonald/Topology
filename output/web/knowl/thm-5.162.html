Theorem 5.5.16.

For each α let Xα be a space with basepoint pα ∈ Xα, and let p := [pα] be the basepoint of the wedge product ∨α Xα. If for each α the singleton space {pα} is a deformation retract of an open set Uα in Xα in X containing pα, then π1(∨α Xα,p) ≅ ∗α π1(Xα,pα) is a free product.
