Let (Xα,𝒯α) be a topological space for each index α ∈ J, and let ∏α Xα be the Cartesian product of the spaces Xα. For each index β, let pβ: ∏α Xα → Xβ be the projection map.
  • The product topology on ∏α Xα is the topology 𝒯prod := 𝒯(𝒮prod) generated by the product subbasis 𝒮prod := { pβ-1(Uβ) | β ∈ J and Uβ ∈ 𝒯β }. The set ∏α Xα with the product topology is called a product space.
  • The box topology on ∏α Xα is the topology 𝒯box := 𝒯(ℬbox) generated by the box basis ℬbox := {∏α Uα | Uα is open in Xα for all indices α}.
