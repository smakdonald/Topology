Proposition 5.6.2.

Let X be a CW complex, let n ≥ 1, and let α ∈ Jn.
  1. The map qn|X(n-1) is an embedding of X(n-1) into X(n).
  2. As a set, X(n) is the disjoint union X(n-1) ∐ (∐α ∈ Jn eαn).
  3. Φα|Sαn-1X(n-1) = φα.
  4. The open n-cell eαn is an open set of X and Φα|Bαneαn: Bαn → eαn is a homeomorphism.
