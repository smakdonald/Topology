Definition 6.2.2.

Given an action of a group G on a space Y, let ∼G be the equivalence relation on Y defined by [y ∼G y' iff there is a g ∈ G with g · y = y'] for all y,y' ∈ Y. For each y ∈ Y, the equivalence class [y] = [y]G = G · y is called the orbit of y. The quotient space Y/∼ is called the orbit space of the group action and denoted Y/G.
