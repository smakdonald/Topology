Theorem 6.1.4.

(LC+ULP = Lifting Criterion and Unique Lifting Property): Let p: (X̃,x̃0) → (X,x0) be a covering space, let Y be a PC and LPC space, and let f: (Y,y0) → (X,x0) be continuous.
  1. If f∗(π1(Y,y0)) ⊆ p∗(π1(X̃,x̃0)), then there is a unique continuous function f̃: (Y,y0) → (X̃,x̃0) such that p ∘ f̃ = f.
  2. If f∗(π1(Y,y0)) ⊈ p∗(π1(X̃,x̃0)), then there does not exist a continuous lift f̃: (Y,y0) → (X̃,x̃0) such that p ∘ f̃ = f.
