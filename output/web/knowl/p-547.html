If p: (X̃,x̃0) → (X,x0) is a covering space, then the induced homomorphism p∗: π1(X̃,x̃0) → π1(X,x0) is injective (that is, Ker p∗ = {1π1(X̃,x̃0)}), and the image satisfies Im p∗ = p∗(π1(X̃,x̃0)) = {[f] ∈ π1(X,x0) | the lift f̃ of f at x̃0 is a loop in X̃}. Consequently, π1(X̃,x̃0) is isomorphic to a subgroup of π1(X,x0).
