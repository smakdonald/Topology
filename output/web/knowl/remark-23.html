Remark 6.3.7.

A Galois correspondence table has columns for spaces, fundamental groups, and covering space group actions. Each row of contains a space X, \pi_1(X), and the (deck transformation) group that acts on X with covering space group action (and is maximal with respect to that property). In the column of spaces, a space Y appears in a row above a space X if there is a covering space : Y \to X.
