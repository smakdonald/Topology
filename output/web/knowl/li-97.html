Item 2.

If ∼ is the smallest equivalence relation on I × I such that (s,1) ∼ (1,1) for all s ∈ I and (0,t) ∼ (1,1) ∼ (1,t) for all t ∈ I, then (I × I)/∼ ≅ D2, with a homeomorphism that maps the equivalence class [(1,1)] to the point (1,0) and maps [(s,0)] to (cos(2πs),sin(2πs)) for all s ∈ I.
