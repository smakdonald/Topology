Let X = [0,1] × [0,1] have the product topology (where each [0,1] has the Euclidean subspace topology).
  1. The torus T2 is the quotient space X/∼ where ∼ is the equivalence relation on X generated by (r,0) ∼ (r,1) and (0,s) ∼ (1,s) for all r,s ∈ [0,1].
  1. The Klein bottle K2 is the quotient space X/∼ where ∼ is the equivalence relation on X generated by (r,0) ∼ (r,1) and (0,s) ∼ (1,1-s) for all r,s ∈ [0,1].
  1. The Mobius band M2 is the quotient space X/∼ where ∼ is the equivalence relation on X generated by (0,s) ∼ (1,1-s) for all s ∈ [0,1].
