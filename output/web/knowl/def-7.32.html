Definition 7.1.11.

Let X be a Δ-complex and let n ≥ 1. The n-th simplicial boundary map is the group homomorphism ∂nΔ = ∂n: CnΔ(X) → Cn-1Δ(X) defined by ∂n(∑α ∈ Jn mασα) := ∑α ∈ Jn mα∂n(1σα) and ∂n(1σα) := ∑i=0n (-1)i σα ∘ Fi(n). The 0-th simplicial boundary map is the group homomorphism ∂0Δ = ∂0: C0Δ(X) → 0.
