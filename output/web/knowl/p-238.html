(CO1) (a) A composition of open functions is open. (b) (CO2) If X_\alpha has the topology T\alpha for each \alpha \in J, and \prod_{\alpha \in J} X_\alpha has the product topology, then the projection map p_\beta : (\prod_{\alpha \in J} X_\alpha ) \to X\beta is open. (c) If f:X \to Y is an open function and if B is a subspace of Y satisfying f(X) \sse B \sse Y, then f|B is an open map. (d) (CO3) A finite product of open maps is open.
