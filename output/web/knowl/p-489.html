  1. If \(f \simeq_p g\) and \(h \simeq_p k\text{,}\) then \(f∗h \simeq_p g∗k\text{.}\)
  2. \(c_{x_0}∗f \simeq_p f \simeq_p f∗c_{x_1}\text{.}\)
  3. \(f∗f_{rev} \simeq_p c_{x_0}\) and \(f_{rev∗} f \simeq p c_{x_1}\text{.}\)
