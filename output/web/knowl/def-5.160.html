Definition 5.5.15.

Let Xα be a topological space for all α in J such that Xβ ∩ Xγ = ∅ for all β ≠ γ ∈ J, and let pα ∈ Xα for all α. Let ∐α Xα be the union of the Xα with the disjoint union topology, and let ∼ be the equivalence relation on ∐α Xα generated by pβ ∼ pγ for all β,γ ∈ J. The quotient space (∐α Xα)/∼ is the wedge product of the spaces ∐α Xα (with respect to the basepoints pα); this space is also denoted ∨α Xα.
