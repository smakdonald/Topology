Definition 4.2.4.

Let X and Y be topological spaces and let f:X → Y be a continuous map. Let (X × I) ∐ Y have the disjoint union topology (where X × I has the product topology), and let ∼ be the minimal equivalence relation on (X × I) ∐ Y such that (p,1) ∼ f(p) for all p ∈ X. The mapping cylinder of f is the quotient space Xf := ((X × I) ∐ Y)/∼.
