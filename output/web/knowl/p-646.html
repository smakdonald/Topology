Let X be a topological space and x0 \in X, and let n ≥ 1 be a natural number. The n-th homotopy group of X at x0, denoted \pi n(X,x0), is the set of homotopy classes of maps (In,Bd\R^n(In)) \to (X,x0) with group operation defined by [f][g] = [f ∗ g] for all [f],[g] \in \pi n(X,x0).
