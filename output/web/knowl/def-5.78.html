Definition 5.4.4.

For a set A, R \sse F(A), and words v,w \in (A \cup A')*, the equation v = w means that v and w are the same word, v =F(A) w means that [v] = [w] in the group F(A), and v =G w means that [v]\langle R \rangle^N = [w]\langle R \rangle^N in the group G := \langle A | R \rangle.
