A topological space is a set \(X\) together with a topology \(𝒯\) on \(X\text{,}\) often denoted \((X,𝒯)\text{.}\) The elements of \(X\) are called points in \(X\text{,}\) and the elements of \(𝒯\) are called open sets in \(X\) or open sets relative to \(X\) or open sets rel \(X\text{.}\)
