Let X and Y be topological spaces and let f:X \to Y be a continuous map. Let (X \times I) ∐ Y have the disjoint union topology (where X \times I has the product topology), and let \sim be the minimal equivalence relation on (X \times I) ∐ Y such that (p,1) \sim f(p) for all p \in X. The mapping cylinder of f is the quotient space Xf := ((X \times I) ∐ Y)/\sim.
