Definition 6.2.1.

Given a group G and a topological space Y, an action of G on Y (or "action of G on Y by homeomorphisms") is a homomorphism ρ:G → Homeo(Y), where Homeo(Y) is the group of homeomorphisms :Y → Y with group operation given by composition. For each g ∈ G and y ∈ Y, denote g · y := (ρ(g))(y).
