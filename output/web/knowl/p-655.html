Motivation: Define homology groups 3 ways: simplicial homology Hn\delta on \delta -complexes, cellular homology HnCW on CW-complexes, and singular homology Hnsing on topological spaces. {\delta -complexes} ⊊ {CW complexes} ⊊ {topological spaces}. Hn\delta is easiest to compute, HnCW is harder to compute but easier to find the complex structure, and Hnsing is hardest to compute but easiest to use for proving theorems. For a \delta -complex X, Hn\delta (X) = HnCW(X) = Hnsing(X). Hn\delta, HnCW, and Hnsing are all homotopy invariants.
