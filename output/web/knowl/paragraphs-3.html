Interactions with constructions and continuous functions:.

Proposition 3.1.7.

  1. If X is a T2 space and Y is a subspace of X, then Y is T2.
  2. If Xα is a T2 space for all α then the product space ∏α Xα is T2.

Remark 3.1.8.

T2 is not preserved by quotients, continuous images, or continuous preimages.
