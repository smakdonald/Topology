Interactions with constructions and continuous functions:.

Proposition 3.1.7.

  1. If \(X\) is a \(T_2\) space and \(Y\) is a subspace of \(X\text{,}\) then \(Y\) is \(T_2\text{.}\)
  2. If \(X_\alpha\) is a \(T_2\) space for all \(\alpha\) then the product space \(\prod_\alpha X_\alpha\) is \(T_2\text{.}\)

Remark 3.1.8.

\(T_2\) is not preserved by quotients, continuous images, or continuous preimages.
