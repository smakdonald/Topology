Let X and Y be topological spaces with X \cap Y = \es, and let x0 \in X and y0 \in Y. Let Z := X \cup Y have the disjoint union topology. Define \sim to be the minimal equivalence relation on Z such that x0 ~ y0, and let Z/\sim be the corresponding quotient space. Then:
  1. There are embeddings X \to Z/\sim and Y \to Z/\sim.
  2. If X and Y are connected, then Z/\sim is connected.
