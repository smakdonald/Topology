Proposition 5.6.5.

Let X be a CW complex.
  1. A subset B of X is closed in X if and only if B ∩ X(n) is closed in X(n) for all n.
  2. If n ≥ 0 and β ∈ Jn, then ClX(eβn) has nonempty intersection with at most finitely many open cell of X.
  3. A subset B of X is closed in X if and only if B ∩ ClX(eβn) is closed in ClX(eβn) for all open cells eβn.
