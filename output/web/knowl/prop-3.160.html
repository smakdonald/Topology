Proposition 3.4.20.

  1. If \approx is the smallest equivalence relation on I such that 0 \approx 1, then I/\approx \cong S^1, with a homeomorphism that maps the equivalence class [s] to the point ( cos(2 \pi s), sin(2 \pi s) ) for all s \in I.
  2. If \sim is the smallest equivalence relation on I \times I such that (s,1) \sim (1,1) for all s \in I and (0,t) \sim (1,1) \sim (1,t) for all t \in I, then (I \times I)/\sim \cong D2, with a homeomorphism that maps the equivalence class [(1,1)] to the point (1,0) and maps [(s,0)] to (cos(2\pi s),sin(2\pi s)) for all s \in I.
