Let \(\langle A | R \rangle\) be a presentation of a group \(G\text{,}\) let \(X̃\) be the Cayley complex, and let \(X\) be the presentation complex. Then
  1. the action of \(G\) on \(X̃\text{,}\) given by \(g · h := (gh), g · eh,a := e(gh),a\text{,}\) and \(g · fh,r := f(gh),r\) for all \(h \in G\text{,}\) \(a\in A\text{,}\) and \(r \in R\text{,}\) is a covering space action;
  2. \(X̃\) is a SC CW complex (and hence PC, LPC, and SLSC), and hence \(\pi_1(X̃/G) \cong G\text{;}\) and
  3. \(X̃/G \cong X\text{.}\)
