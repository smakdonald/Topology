Let \langle A | R \rangle be a presentation of a group G, let X̃ be the Cayley complex, and let X be the presentation complex. Then
  1. the action of G on X̃, given by g · h := (gh), g · eh,a := e(gh),a, and g · fh,r := f(gh),r for all h \in G, a\in A, and r \in R, is a covering space action;
  2. X̃ is a SC CW complex (and hence PC, LPC, and SLSC), and hence \pi_1(X̃/G) \cong G; and
  3. X̃/G \cong X.
