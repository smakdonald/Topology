Euclidean topology on \(ℝ^n: 𝒯_{Eucl} := \{U ⊆ ℝ | \text{for all} p ∈ U \text{there is an} r > 0 \text{and} q ∈ ℝ^n \text{such that} p ∈ B(q,r) ⊆ U \}\text{,}\) where \(B(q,r) = B_n(q,r) := \{x ∈ ℝ^n | d_{Eucl}(q,x) < r\}\) is the ball of radius \(r\) centered at q (and \(d_Eucl\) is Euclidean distance).
