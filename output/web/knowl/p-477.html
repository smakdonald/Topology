Let X be a disjoint union of singleton spaces Vα = {vα} (for α ∈ J) and spaces Eβ (for β ∈ K) homeomorpic to I. For each β ∈ K, let sβ,tβ ∈ J. Let ∼ be the equivalence relation on X generated by the relation 0β ∼ vsβ and 1β ∼ vtβ. The quotient space X/∼ is a graph. Each point [vα] is a vertex of the graph X/∼, and the image of each Eβ is an edge of the graph.
