Definition 7.2.2.

Let X be a topological space and let n \geq 0. Let Sn be the set of singular n-simplices of X. The group of singular n-chains of X is Cnsing(X) = Cn(X) := ⊕singular n-simplices \sigma \Z = ⊕singular n-simplices \sigma \Z \sigma . An element of Cnsing(X) is called a (singular) n-chain, and written ∑singular n-simplices \sigma m\sigma \sigma , where each m\sigma \in \Z and all but finitely many m\sigma 's are 0.
