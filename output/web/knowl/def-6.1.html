Definition 6.1.1.

A covering space of a topological space X is a space X̃ together with a continuous function p: X̃ → X satisfying: There is an open covering {Uα} of X such that for all α the preimage p-1(Uα) is a disjoint union of open sets in X̃, each of which is mapped (by the restriction of p) homeomorphically onto Uα. Each set Uα is called evenly covered.
