Proposition 2.2.27.

  1. CC12-i.
    If \(X/\sim\) is a quotient space of \(X\) and \(q: X \to X/\sim\) is the equivalence map (defined by \(q(p) := [p]\) for all \(p \in X\)), then \(q\) is a continuous surjection.
  2. The quotient topology induced by an equivalence relation is the largest topology on the set of equivalence classes for which the equivalence map is continuous.
